Who We Are

The Voice of Those Who Suffer in Silence

The **Ali Horse Rescues** project was born out of outrage at the abandonment and neglect suffered by urban and rural horses. Noble, strong animals that gave their strength to human labor and end up discarded and injured on the streets.

Our work consists of removing these horses from situations of extreme danger, providing emergency medical care from qualified veterinarians, essential medications, specific nutritious feed, and a temporary home of peace and dignity.

We believe that every rescued horse deserves a second chance to live free from pain and feel the affection they never received.
